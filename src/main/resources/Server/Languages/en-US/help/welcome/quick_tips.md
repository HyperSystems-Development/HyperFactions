# Quick Tips

## Claiming Land
`/f claim`
Protects the chunk you're standing in.

## Faction Home
`/f home`
Teleports to your faction home. Set with /f sethome.

## Faction Chat
`/f c`
Cycles chat mode: Normal > Faction > Ally.

> Dying costs power, weakening your territory hold!
